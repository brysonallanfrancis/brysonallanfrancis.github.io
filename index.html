About Us:

Here at the American Heart Association, our goal has been to reduce disability and death from cardiovascular disease and strokes since 1924. We seek to educate people on heart healthy lifestyles. We raise awareness toward the preventive measures that can be taken and fund research that aims to better understand heart disease and strokes. We offer training in CPR, first aid, and advanced cardiopulmonary resuscitation. We also advocate for policies that promote heart health on the local, state, and national levels.
