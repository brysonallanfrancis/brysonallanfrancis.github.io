CPR Basics

Cardiopulmonary resuscitation (CPR) is used when someone's breathing and/or heartbeat has reduced. Here are the basic steps to perform CPR:

  1. Ensure the area is safe from danger.
  2. Check the person's responsivness before performing CPR.
  3. If the person is unresponsive, call 911 immediately.
  4. Check person's breathing. If patterns seem unordinary perfrom CPR.
  5. Push hard and fast on the center of the chest at a rate of 100 to 120 compressions per minute.
  6. If capable perform resuce breaths.
  7. Continue CPR until the emergecny response team arrives.
