Beginner Exercise Plan

Introduction

Welcome to the Beginner Exercise Plan! This plan is designed to help you ease into a regular exercise routine and build a foundation for a healthier lifestyle.

Warm-up

Start with a 10-minute brisk walk to get your blood flowing and warm up your muscles.

Cardio

After the warm-up, perform 20 minutes of low-intensity cardio such as walking, cycling, or swimming.

Strength Training

Follow the cardio with 10 minutes of bodyweight exercises, including squats, lunges, push-ups, and planks.

Cool Down

Finish the workout with a 5-minute cool-down, consisting of gentle stretching exercises to help prevent muscle soreness.

Frequency

Try to do this workout routine 3 times a week, with at least one rest day in between sessions.

Remember to listen to your body and modify the exercises as needed. Consult with a healthcare professional before starting any new exercise program.
